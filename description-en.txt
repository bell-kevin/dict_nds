Low German Spelling Dictionary

After installation, mark your text as Low German:
Menu Tools->Language->all text
Then pick Low German in the appearing dialog.

Further infos:
  http://lern-platt.de/plattdeutsche-rechtschreibpruefung/
  http://platt.knabbe.net/
